Clinical Services

Evidence-based psychological support tailored to your unique emotional needs, fostering healing and secure connections.

Individual Psychotherapy

Life can sometimes present challenges that are difficult to navigate alone. Individual psychotherapy offers a secure, confidential space to explore your inner world, process difficult emotions, and develop healthy coping mechanisms.

My clinical approach integrates evidence-based practices with a deep understanding of human attachment and emotional processing. Together, we can address:

  • Anxiety, panic, and chronic stress
  • Depression and feelings of emptiness
  • Trauma and past emotional wounds
  • Life transitions, grief, and loss
  • Self-esteem and identity issues

The goal is to move beyond simply managing symptoms toward experiencing true emotional freedom and a deeper sense of self.

Couples Therapy (EFT)

When communication breaks down, relationships can become a source of profound pain and loneliness. Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT) is a highly effective, empirically validated approach designed to help couples identify and break negative interaction cycles.

Instead of just teaching communication "skills," EFT focuses on the root cause of relational distress: the threat to our secure attachment bond. In our sessions, we will work to:

  • De-escalate repetitive arguments and conflicts
  • Identify the underlying emotions driving your partner's behavior
  • Rebuild broken trust and heal attachment injuries
  • Foster deep emotional responsiveness and accessibility

Whether you are in a crisis or simply looking to deepen your connection, EFT provides a roadmap to a more loving, secure partnership.

Clinical Assessment

A comprehensive psychological evaluation is often the crucial first step in creating an effective treatment plan. Through standardized clinical interviews and validated psychometric tools, we can gain a clearer understanding of your mental health landscape.

Assessments are tailored to the individual and may include:

  • Diagnostic evaluations for mood and anxiety disorders
  • Personality assessments to understand pervasive behavioral patterns
  • Trauma and stressor-related evaluations

The results of these assessments are treated with the utmost confidentiality and are used collaboratively to ensure that the therapeutic approach we choose is the most effective one for your specific needs.
