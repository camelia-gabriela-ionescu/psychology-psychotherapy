My Professional Journey

Hello, and welcome. I am Camelia-Gabriela Ionescu, a licensed Clinical Psychologist and Psychotherapist practicing in Romania. I have always had a 'detective's curiosity' about the inner worlds of people. My fascination with the motives behind our actions made me feel very much at home studying psychology, and I approach psychotherapy with that same methodical precision and care.

I focus on clinical assessment and evidence-based interventions to help others understand their own 'whys.' For me, therapy is about creating meaning and mapping the journey from early relationships to adult resilience.

My Therapeutic Philosophy

I believe that healing does not happen in isolation. We are fundamentally social beings, wired for connection. When those connections are threatened or broken, we experience deep psychological distress. This belief is the cornerstone of my practice.

My therapeutic approach is deeply grounded in attachment, and emotion theories - alongside advances in neuroscience. I specialize in Emotion-Focused Therapy (EFT), a highly effective, evidence-based therapeutic approach primarily used for individuals, but also applicable to couples, teens and families. Instead of just teaching communication skills, EFT guides you to directly explore, shift, and improve the underlying emotions that keep you stuck, leading to real and lasting positive change by using the highly supportive, safe relationship developed during sessions.

What to Expect in Our Sessions

Entering therapy can feel daunting, but you will not be navigating it alone. I strive to create an environment that is warm, entirely non-judgmental, and collaboratively paced. My role is not to "fix" you, but to act as a secure base and a guide as we map out your emotional landscape together.

In our sessions, we will work to identify negative patterns of interaction, make sense of overwhelming emotions, and cultivate the self-compassion necessary for true healing. Our shared goal will be to foster a deeper sense of authenticity, security, and vitality in your daily life.
