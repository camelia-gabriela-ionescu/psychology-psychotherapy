My Professional Journey

Hello, and welcome. I am Camelia-Gabriela Ionescu, a licensed Clinical Psychologist and psychotherapist practicing in Romania. My journey into the field of psychology was driven by a profound curiosity about human resilience and the intricate ways our early relationships shape our adult lives.

I completed my formal education with a focus on clinical assessment and evidence-based interventions. Over the years, I have had the privilege of working with individuals and couples navigating a wide spectrum of emotional difficulties, from acute anxiety and depression to profound relational disconnects and trauma.

My Therapeutic Philosophy

I believe that healing does not happen in isolation. We are fundamentally social beings, wired for connection. When those connections are threatened or broken, we experience deep psychological distress. This belief is the cornerstone of my practice.

My therapeutic approach is deeply grounded in Attachment Theory. I specialize in Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT), an empirically validated model that looks beneath the surface of behavioral symptoms to address the core emotional needs and attachment fears driving them. Rather than simply teaching you how to "manage" your symptoms or "argue better" with your partner, my goal is to help you restructure your emotional responses and build secure, lasting bonds.

What to Expect in Our Sessions

Entering therapy can feel daunting, but you will not be navigating it alone. I strive to create an environment that is warm, entirely non-judgmental, and collaboratively paced. My role is not to "fix" you, but to act as a secure base and a guide as we map out your emotional landscape together.

In our sessions, we will work to identify negative cycles of interaction, make sense of overwhelming emotions, and cultivate the self-compassion necessary for true healing. Whether we are working individually or as a couple, our shared goal will be to foster a deeper sense of authenticity, security, and vitality in your daily life.
