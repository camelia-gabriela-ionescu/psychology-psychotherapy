Meet Camelia

I am a licensed Clinical Psychologist and certified Emotion-Focused Therapy (EFT) practitioner, with over 18 years of experience in the field of psychological testing, evaluation, and clinical profiling.

My professional journey has been defined by a commitment to bridging the gap between rigorous scientific psychometrics and empathetic, person-centered care.

By combining both, deep clinical insight with standardized psychometric tools, my practice empowers you to move beyond mere symptom relief—equipping you with the emotional tools to understand your patterns, build lasting resilience, and lead a more authentic, connected life.
